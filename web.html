Cal Lodge

The Cal Lodge is a historic building built by U.C. student volunteers and owned by the Associated Students of the University of California.


I redesigned the Cal Lodge website to be more easily navigable and understandable by breaking up the site information into different sections and emphasizing the booking-related pages. I also emphasizes the lodge's tie with UC Berkeley by using official Cal colors and logos.
